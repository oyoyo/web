Why this edition of FontoXML?

Make structured content authoring accessible for anyone! That is our mission at FontoXML.

This means a few things:

  • we strive to be the most user friendly XML editor in the world;

  • we are constantly looking for ways to make 'getting started' with structured authoring as easy as possible.

This version of FontoXML is aimed to do precisely that; we want you to get your hands on FontoXML to get a feel of the possibilities, using your own content. You can use your cloud-storage like Google Drive or Microsoft OneDrive to start working directly.

Enjoy the ride!
